Image Formats

There are four main image formats in use on the web, they are all designed to do different things. Understanding their intended purpose goes a long way towards improving the quality of your web pages.

JPG images are good for photos

GIFs are good for simple animations

PNGs are good for diagrams and icons

SVGs are amazing. Use them wherever you can
